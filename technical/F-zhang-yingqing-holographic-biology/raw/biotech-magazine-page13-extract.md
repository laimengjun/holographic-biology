## 英文正文（Page 13）

**Paving the Way for Modernisation of Traditional Chinese Medicine**

Modernisation of traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) would help its further development and international implementation. The Holographic Biological Law states that micro-systems or miniature biological units are miniature representatives of the whole organism. Interviewing the Chief Physician Du Chang-hua of the Bao Shan TCM Hospital in Shanghai serves as a precious and first-hand input on this new biological theory. Biological cells are "omni-potent" — they contain the full copy of the genetic code for the whole biological organism so that appropriate multiplication would lead to a copy of the whole organism.

In line with this, this new Law studies the scientific basis of TCM methods for diagnosis and treatment, e.g., the observation of tongue changes would accurately reflect parallel changes of the whole body; acupuncture on different parts of the ear lobe has proven effect on different organs in the body.

The new Law would not only elucidate the sound scientific basis of TCM but would also assist the prevailing analytical scientific study methods of body functions. Moreover, an important application is the Holographic Diagnostic Method to detect body malfunctioning by noting micro-system bio-physical changes, e.g., those of skin electrical resistance of parts of the ear lobe.
